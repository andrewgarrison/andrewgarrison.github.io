Background

Although, I did not get into web design/coding until I was a junior in high school. I started out video editing at the age of 12 and from there I moved on to 3D design, audio editing, and some photo editing. I'm by no means an expert or skilled at any of the methods I just listed, they were all done for enjoyment. This is the first website I've ever made, and I hope there will be many more to come!
